How to play

Guess every word in the mini crossword. All the words fit the day's theme — that's your only starting hint!

  • Tap any square to pick a word (tap again on a crossing to switch direction).
  • Type a guess for the whole word and hit Enter.
  • A right letter, right spot — it locks into the grid. On later guesses, just type the whole word naturally: locked letters soak up their keystroke without changing.
  • B in the word, but a different spot. It lingers in amber right where you tried it — and tiny pencil marks keep the full history of every spot you've tested it — so you never try the same place twice. Just type over them.
  • C not in this word at all.

The keyboard remembers what you've learned for each word separately — switch words and it switches too. Locked letters carry across crossings, so every solve helps its neighbors. Stuck? Tap Hint for a clue.
